LeftPad and Go: can tooling help?
Source: https://divan.github.io/posts/leftpad_and_go/

Nice article about the mindset of Golang community regarding DRY.

A little copying is better than a little dependency.

The article also promotes a simple tool created by the author to identify small dependencies, DepsCheck. The tool was the first contact of the author with Golang AST (Abstract Syntax Tree), in the article he describes how pleasant was to deal with go/{ast,parser,types,loader} packages. The whole tool was the work of a weekend.
